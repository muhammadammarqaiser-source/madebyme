POINT TO POINT & POINT TO MULTIPOINT FIBER & WIRELESS CONNECTIVITY

Mcsol provides high-speed Point-to-Point (P2P) and Point-to-Multipoint (P2MP) connectivity through both Fiber optics and wireless solutions, catering to specific needs and constraints.

DARK CORE CONNECTIVITY OVER OPTICAL FIBER

Mcsol offers specialized networking architecture utilizing dark Fiber strands for high-speed, dedicated communication links. We provide Dark Core connec- tivity and lease unlit Fiber throughout Faisalabad and its suburbs.

DATA CENTRE & SERVER CO-LOCATION SERVICES

• Infrastructure Hosting • Managed Services • Connectivity Services • Environmental (Temperature) Control • Uninterrupted Power with Multi-layer Backup • Physical Security

MCSOL ISP SERVICES

FTTH (Fiber to the Home) Mcsol offers high-speed broadband services in Faisalabad and its suburbs. We provide unlimited data packages with unparalleled internet speeds at affordable rates, covering the entire region with our cutting-edge underground Optical Fiber network, supported by Transworld.

BANDWIDTH & CDN SERVICES

We fulfill bandwidth requirements, whether raw or mixed with CDNs, dealing in both asymmetric and symmetric bandwidths. Wherever you need it in Pakistan, we've got you covered.

DEALER'S PANEL ISP SERVICES

Our Dealer Panel ISP Services, powered by Transworld, PTCL, and Zong, offer robust unlimited data packages with lightning-fast internet speeds. We proudly maintain a 99.9% uptime, ensuring the best services across Pakistan.
